A Visitor in the Yard

Produced by Brenda Maina

Read the full transcript

Audio: birds chirping continuously throughout.

[00:00] A small bird is already perched on a branch, eating from a patch of fungus growing on the wood.

[00:04] It takes a bite, then pauses to look around the yard.

[00:08] Another bird lands on a branch in the background.

[00:12] The bird keeps eating, glancing around after each bite.

[00:16] A third bird flits onto a nearby branch as the first one continues feeding.

Filmed in a backyard in Colombia, this short story captures a single unguarded moment — a bird feeding while staying alert to its surroundings, with other visitors passing through the same branches.

StoryCast includes moments like this alongside longer narrated pieces, because not every story needs words to be worth paying attention to.
