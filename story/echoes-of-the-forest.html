Echoes of the Forest

Produced by Brenda Maina

Read the full transcript

[00:00] Sunlight filters through the forest canopy as the day begins.

[00:04] Birdsong rises in the early morning quiet, layered and overlapping.

[00:08] A gentle breeze moves through the leaves above.

[00:12] Somewhere in the distance, water trickles over stone.

This story follows the rainforest as it wakes — the slow shift from stillness to a chorus of birds, insects, and wind. It's a reminder of how much sound carries in a place most of us will never visit in person.

StoryCast captures moments like this to bring listeners closer to environments and experiences outside their own.
