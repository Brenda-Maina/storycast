About StoryCast

Our Mission

StoryCast exists to amplify voices that don't always get heard. We believe storytelling — in sound and image — can connect people across distance and difference.

From rainforest soundscapes to quiet backyard moments, every story here is chosen for what it reveals about a place, a person, or a moment.

Accessibility Commitment

  • Every video includes captions via a <track> element.
  • Every story includes a full, readable transcript.
  • The site meets WCAG 2.1 AA contrast and keyboard-navigation standards.
  • Semantic HTML is used throughout so screen readers can navigate the structure reliably.
