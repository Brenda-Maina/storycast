About StoryCast

We believe every voice deserves to be heard — by everyone. StoryCast makes immersive audio and video storytelling accessible from the ground up.

Our Mission

StoryCast exists to amplify voices that don't always get heard. We believe storytelling — in sound and image — can connect people across distance and difference.

From rainforest soundscapes to quiet backyard moments, every story here is chosen for what it reveals about a place, a person, or a moment in time.

Accessibility Commitment

  • 📝 Every video includes captions via a <track> element.
  • 📄 Every story includes a full, readable transcript.
  • 🎨 All colour combinations meet WCAG 2.1 AA contrast requirements.
  • ⌨️ Full keyboard navigation including media keyboard shortcuts.
  • 🌙 Dark mode available, saving your preference across visits.

How to Engage

  1. Browse Stories

    Use the filter buttons on the homepage to find Audio or Video stories. Each card shows the type and a short description.

  2. Listen or Watch

    Click any story card to open the full story page. The media player loads automatically — press play or use the Space bar.

  3. Read Along

    Expand the transcript below the player. As the media plays, the current line highlights automatically so you can follow along.

  4. Share

    Use the Share button on any story page to send it to someone. The link copies to your clipboard if your device doesn't support sharing.
